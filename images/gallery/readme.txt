Drop your photos into this folder using these exact filenames
(one per gallery slot, 16 total, in whatever order you like):
 
  gallery-01.jpg   gallery-05.jpg   gallery-09.jpg   gallery-13.jpg
  gallery-02.jpg   gallery-06.jpg   gallery-10.jpg   gallery-14.jpg
  gallery-03.jpg   gallery-07.jpg   gallery-11.jpg   gallery-15.jpg
  gallery-04.jpg   gallery-08.jpg   gallery-12.jpg   gallery-16.jpg
 
Notes:
- JPG or PNG both work — just keep the .jpg extension in the filename,
  or edit the src/data-full in index.html to match if you use .png/.webp.
- Recommended: compress each photo to roughly 150-300 KB, ~1200px on the
  longest side. Squarish crops (1:1) look best since each tile is a square.
- If you have fewer than 16 photos, delete the unused <button
  class="gal-item gal-photo" ...> blocks in index.html (search for
  "gallery-XX.jpg" to find each one) rather than leaving broken image icons.
- The "Training & Certification" section further up the homepage (First
  Aid, Working at Height, Risk Assessment, Fire Fighting cards) is
  separate from this gallery and was left untouched — no action needed
  there.
