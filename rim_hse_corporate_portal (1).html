About RIM-HSE

Bridging International Standards with Local Operations

RIM-HSE (Réseau d'Ingénierie Mauritanien pour la Santé, la Sécurité et l'Environnement) is an established professional service company that champions international workplace safety cultures in Northwest Africa. By providing specialized training courses, system auditing, hazard identification, and advanced machinery inspections, we help both private firms and government institutions lower environmental risks and operational liabilities.

Since 2017, we have worked closely with industry leaders in mining, logistics, energy, and telecommunications to establish robust safety guidelines, train thousands of personnel, and verify regulatory compliance.
